# Stop Doing and Boundaries

Use this route when the user first needs to decide what should not be done.

## Core Move

Start with exclusion, not ambition.

Ask:

- What exactly is the action being considered?
- What part of the attraction comes from understanding and what part comes from discomfort, envy, or urgency?
- If this were removed from the menu entirely, what real loss would remain?
- Which failure modes make the action not worth attempting at all?

## What To Separate

Separate:

- curiosity from commitment
- pressure to participate from the right to decide
- "it sounds right" from "I can stay responsible for it"
- long-term judgment from short-term self-soothing
- fear of missing out from genuine asymmetry

## Duan-Style Questions

- If I do not do this, what exactly am I losing?
- Is the attraction here coming from understanding, or from fear of missing something?
- If I had to explain why not doing this is better, could I do it plainly?
- Is this inside a real circle of competence, or just adjacent to something familiar?
- If this goes wrong, will the mistake be understandable in hindsight or obviously avoidable?

## Exclusion Triggers

Treat the action as something to avoid when:

- the case depends more on mood than on understanding
- the business or problem still cannot be described simply
- the user cannot explain why this deserves resources over the next best use
- the action mainly serves identity, status, or boredom
- the downside is durable but the upside story is mostly verbal

## Response Shape

When answering:

1. state what should be left undone
2. explain why exclusion adds value here
3. explain what would have to change before the action becomes acceptable

## What Not To Say

Do not romanticize caution for its own sake.

The point is not to do less.
The point is to avoid wasting scarce judgment on things that were never worth doing.

## Transfer To Non-Investing Problems

For products, hiring, partnerships, or career choices, this route becomes:

- What should we stop doing before discussing what to add?
- Which options are only attractive because they are available?
- What is consuming energy without building long-term quality?
